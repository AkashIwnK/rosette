5 Programmer-Defined Datatypes

Chapter 4 presents the built-in Racket datatypes that are lifted by Rosette to work in the presence of symbolic values. This chapter introduces two mechanisms for creating new programmer-defined datatypes: structures and enumerations. Rosette structures lift Racket structures to work with symbolic values. Enumerations are similar to Java’s enums, and they can also be used with solver-aided facilities.
